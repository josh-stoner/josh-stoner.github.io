klikWheel — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 3, 2026

klikWheel is a local music player for iOS. Your privacy is simple here: klikWheel does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

Your music stays on your device

Audio files and artwork you import are stored only in the app's sandbox on your iPhone. They are never uploaded, synced, or sent anywhere.

No accounts, no tracking, no analytics

klikWheel has no login, collects no usage data, contains no third-party advertising or analytics SDKs, and makes no network requests to us.

Purchases

The optional Tip Jar (and the bonus themes it unlocks) is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see or receive your payment information. Apple's handling of those transactions is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

Your data rights

Because no data is collected, there is nothing for us to access, correct, or delete.

Changes

If this policy changes — for example, if a future version adds Apple Music streaming — this page will be updated and the date above revised.
