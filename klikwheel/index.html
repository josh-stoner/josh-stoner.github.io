klikWheel

A music player you actually touch. Spin to scroll, press to play — tactile wheel with haptic clicks. Your library or Apple Music, no accounts required.

Download on the App Store

What it does

🎛

Tactile scroll wheel

Drag the wheel to scroll, tap a zone to act. Every tick has a haptic click — deliberate, physical control over your music.

🎵

Your library, offline

Import MP3, FLAC, and more. Your files live in the app's private storage — no cloud, no sync, nothing leaves your iPhone.

☁️

Apple Music streaming

Search and stream the full Apple Music catalog alongside your local files, through the same warm retro interface.

📡

AirPlay

Send audio to any AirPlay speaker or TV straight from the Now Playing screen. No extra steps.

🎨

9 collectible themes

Classic silver, midnight black, rose, sage, and more. A handful of bonus themes unlock via the optional Tip Jar.

♿

Built for everyone

Full VoiceOver support, Dynamic Type, and WCAG AA contrast on every theme. Zero ads, zero tracking, zero accounts.

Free

No subscriptions. An optional Tip Jar ($0.99 / $2.99 / $4.99) unlocks a few bonus themes — nothing else is behind a paywall. Apple Music streaming requires an active Apple Music subscription.
