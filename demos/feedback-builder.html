The redesign thesis
The original guide is built for comprehensiveness — the right goal for a training artifact. But it fails the manager who has 5 minutes before a hard conversation. They don't need to read about feedback. They need to give it.

This redesign converts the same framework into a decision-support tool: scenario-based, question-driven, built to live where feedback actually happens — in Slack and Confluence, not on a page you have to find first.
